Welcome to Hippos Information

Hippos are amazing creatures that are found in Africa. They are known for their large size and semi-aquatic lifestyle. There are two species of hippos: the common hippo and the pygmy hippo. On this website, you will find information about hippos, their habitat, behavior, and conservation efforts.

Explore our website to learn more about these fascinating animals!
